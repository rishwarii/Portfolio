Most AI prototypes fail during the transition to production because they lack observability.

My rule: if a behavior matters, it needs a metric. If a metric matters, it needs a trace path to source inputs, retrieval context, and generation settings.

A platform mindset starts when iteration speed is matched by debuggability.
