Reliable assistants are not only model problems. They are systems problems.

I design guardrails as layers:
1. Retrieval quality and document freshness
2. Generation boundaries and refusal behavior
3. Product-level messaging when uncertainty is high

This stack creates better outcomes than prompt tuning alone, especially in domains where users assume high authority from the interface.
